Venue Overview

Set atop the picturesque cliffs of Santorini, this luxurious destination offers panoramic views of the caldera and the Aegean Sea. Blending traditional Cycladic architecture with contemporary design, it’s the perfect setting for your dream wedding.

  • Capacity: 150 Guests
  • 40 Luxury Suites
  • Private Dining & Bar Lounge
  • In-house Decor & Floral Setup
  • Live Music & DJ Arrangements
  • Professional Photography & Drone Coverage
  • Beach & Cliff Ceremony Options

Wedding Packages

  • Classic Romance: Ceremony setup, decor, and 2-night couple stay
  • Luxury Dream: Decor, 3-day event planning, photography & dinner
  • Royal Grandeur: Full venue buyout, custom theme, entertainment, and gourmet dining
